Wellcome to DSP GRADUATE ATTRIBUTES

DSP GRADUATE ATTRIBUTES - "Guided by God's truth, we serve to lead and build community."

GOD CENTERED

- one who has a strong faith to the divine being manifested to he or she lives according to the image and likeness of God has intentions shows willfulness discretion, loves, communicates, testifies, teaches, and prays. The main element of the design shows a praying hand, and a cross

SERVANT LEADER

- One who serves and exemplifies Christ's leadership as lived by St. Dominic, St. Catherine, and Mother Francisca del Espiritu Santo de Fuentes. Servant leadership is a leadership philosophy in which the main goal of the leader is to serve. it entails a set of practice enriches the lives of individuals, builds better organization and ultimately creates and more just caring world.

COMMUNITY BUILDER

- One who can make changes that matter as long as he or she is purposeful and consistent to foster trust and build strong relationships at every step of the way in achieving success. He or she demonstrates the ability to work with a diverse group of people for a common good.

TRUTH SEEKERS

- One who seeks " veritas "(which means the truth) through assiduous study across disciplines. The main element of the design shows a hand with an open book that symbolizes learning and knowledge.
